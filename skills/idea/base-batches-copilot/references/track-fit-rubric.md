# Base Batches — Track Fit Rubric

Score the team against each track. The track with the highest fit score wins; ties go to where the team is most resourced to compete.

## How to score

For each criterion, assign 0 / 1 / 2:
- 0 = does not fit / not present
- 1 = partial / unclear
- 2 = strong fit

Sum per track. Highest sum wins. Hard disqualifiers (DQ) override the sum — if any DQ row triggers, that track is out regardless of score.

## Startup track

| Criterion | DQ if | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Stage | Already raised seed | Mainnet & users | Prototype | Idea+demo |
| Funding | Raised > ~$250k | $200–250k raised | $50–200k raised | < $50k or none |
| Team | — | Solo, no prior shipping | Solo, prior shipping OR pair | 2+ shipped together |
| Why Base | "Cheap and fast" only | Vague | Names a primitive | Primitive + integration shown |
| Demo | No demo planned | Mockups | Prototype | Live demo URL |
| Investability | — | No clear ask | Generic ask | Specific ask + traction signal |

**Strong fit**: ≥ 8 / 12.
**Apply if**: ≥ 6 and no DQ.

## Student track

| Criterion | DQ if | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| All undergrads? | Any non-undergrad on team | — | Mixed | All undergrads |
| Technical skill | — | Beginner | One coder | Multiple shipped projects |
| Why Base | "Cheap and fast" only | Vague | Names a primitive | Primitive + integration shown |
| Demo | No demo | Designs | Prototype | Working demo |
| Originality | — | Common idea | Twist on common | Genuinely novel |

**Strong fit**: ≥ 7 / 10.

## Robotics track

| Criterion | DQ if | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Robotics expertise | No relevant background | Hobbyist | One member with experience | Multiple with shipped robotics |
| Onchain hook | No reasonable Base/onchain story | Forced fit | Plausible | Tightly integrated |
| Build feasibility in 4 weeks | Cannot reasonably ship in cohort | Ambitious | Tight | Realistic |
| Demo | No physical demo possible | Sim only | Hardware demo planned | Working hardware |

**Strong fit**: ≥ 6 / 8.

## When in doubt

Apply to the track where:
1. You're not DQ'd, and
2. The selection signal matches your strengths.

A solo undergrad with a great demo: Student track, not Startup.
A funded team with 18 months of background: Startup, not Student (even if some are undergrads).
A robotics PhD with a Base agent for fleet payments: Robotics, not Startup.

## Disqualifiers — common foot-guns

- Applying to Student with a non-undergrad on the team.
- Applying to Startup having raised > ~$250k.
- Applying to Robotics without robotics experience or interest signal.
- Applying multiple tracks with the same project (typically not allowed; check the rules).
