# Demo Day Script (3 minutes)

How to record a Base Batches Demo Day pitch — or the backup video. Ceiling: 3 minutes. Demo Day audiences include investors, Base team, and partners — pitch accordingly.

## Structure

### 0:00–0:20 — Hook (20s)
- One sentence: who has the problem, what changes for them.
- Visual that makes the problem real (a screenshot, a stat, a tweet).
- Avoid "Hi I'm X." Open with the problem.

### 0:20–0:40 — Solution (20s)
- One sentence: what your product does.
- Quick architecture or flow diagram (5 seconds on screen, then back to product).

### 0:40–2:00 — Live Demo (80s)
- The actual product. Click through, narrate.
- Show one core flow end-to-end.
- For Base products: show the **Base-specific moment** — Smart Wallet onboarding, the Mini App in Farcaster, the OnchainKit component, the Basename, the Coinbase Verification, the x402 payment, etc.
- If wallet UI shows a Basename, zoom in. That's a strong frame.
- Show the Basescan link briefly as proof.

### 2:00–2:30 — Why Base + Why Now (30s)
- Name the primitive. "We could not build this without {{X}}."
- One sentence on why this is the right moment.

### 2:30–2:50 — Traction (20s)
- Real numbers. Users, transactions, design partners.
- If pre-traction, name the next milestone and the date.

### 2:50–3:00 — Ask (10s)
- What you want from the audience.
- "We're hiring our first contract engineer. Reach me at @handle."

## Recording tips

- **1080p**, decent mic, quiet room.
- **Browser**: clean profile, no bookmarks bar, no other tabs.
- **Pace**: slightly slower than natural conversation.
- **Cuts**: trim every dead second. 3:00 should feel full, not loose.
- **No script-reading on camera**. Bullet points only.
- If the product loads slowly, **cut the wait** in the edit.

## Tools

- Recording: OBS, Loom, QuickTime
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve (free), iMovie
- Captions: auto-generate then proofread; many investors watch muted on first pass

## Anti-patterns

- Starting with "Hi, I'm…" (start with the problem).
- Showing your terminal for more than 10 seconds.
- Apologizing for bugs (cut around them).
- Reading from a script (use bullet points).
- Going over 3:00 (audience stops watching).
- Pitching the roadmap instead of the product (the product is what investors fund).
